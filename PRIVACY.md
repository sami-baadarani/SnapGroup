# SnapGroup Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-06_

SnapGroup is built to respect your privacy completely.

- **No data collection.** SnapGroup does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.
- **Window titles stay on your Mac.** Window titles are held in memory only during the current session to show what's in each group. They are never written to disk or sent over the network.
- **No analytics or tracking.** There is no analytics, telemetry, advertising, or crash reporting of any kind.
- **Local preferences only.** Your hotkey settings are stored locally via macOS `UserDefaults` and never leave your device.

## Network access

SnapGroup makes **one** kind of network request: checking for software updates via [Sparkle](https://sparkle-project.org). This fetches the app's public release feed (`appcast.xml`) and, if you choose to update, downloads the new version — both hosted on GitHub. No personal information is sent with these requests.

## Accessibility permission

SnapGroup requires macOS Accessibility permission to move and focus windows. It is used solely to manage your windows on your behalf — nothing about your windows is collected or transmitted.

## GDPR / international

Because SnapGroup collects no personal data, no consent banners, data-processing agreements, or DPIA are required under GDPR or comparable regulations.
